Frequently Asked Questions

How does AnsibleDB2 help my organisation pass audits?
AnsibleDB2 captures every fact collected during playbook runs and stores it in a queryable, immutable timeline. Auditors receive pre-built reports showing patch compliance, change approvals, and remediation evidence—all mapped to the control frameworks you select.
Can AnsibleDB2 reduce the time my team spends on automation maintenance?
Yes. Playbooks no longer need to gather expensive data repeatedly. Your automation references AnsibleDB2 for current state, while continuous collectors keep information up to date in the background. Runbooks execute faster and engineering hours are focused on delivering change, not data collection.
What cost savings can we expect?
Organisations commonly realise value in three areas: fewer audit findings, lower infrastructure support labour, and reduced spend on redundant CMDB or compliance tooling. With policy-driven retention controls you only store the data you need, optimising infrastructure and licensing costs.
Does AnsibleDB2 integrate with our existing ecosystem?
AnsibleDB2 ships with APIs, webhook triggers, and native exporters for SIEM, GRC, and ITSM platforms. You can trigger corrective playbooks when drift is detected, or feed insights into dashboards like Grafana and PowerBI for executive visibility.

Quick Start Checklist

  • Install the AnsibleDB2 collection and enable the fact persistence plugin.
  • Define retention and access policies aligned with your compliance obligations.
  • Connect collectors for cloud, OS, and middleware platforms.
  • Publish dashboards and audit-ready reports to stakeholders.
